IMS 2.0 is a full-scale Retail Operating System designed for multi-store optical retail businesses.

This is NOT just a POS.
It covers end-to-end operations including:
- Sales (POS)
- Inventory & Stock Movement
- Clinical / Optometry workflows
- HR, Attendance, Salary & Incentives
- Finance & Compliance
- Tasks & SOPs
- AI-powered Intelligence (read-only, Superadmin governed)
- Marketplaces (Shopify, Amazon, Flipkart)
- Expenses & Advances
- Training, Guidelines & Rollout control

This repository currently contains **skeleton structure only**.
All business logic, workflows, and permissions are defined in `/docs/SYSTEM_INTENT.md`.

No implementation assumptions should be made outside SYSTEM_INTENT.md.

Target users:
- Store Staff
- Optometrists
- Store Managers
- HQ Teams
- Finance & Accounts
- Admins
- Superadmins

This repo is designed to be imported into Emergent for UI-first development.
