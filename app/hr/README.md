# HR, Attendance & Payroll

This module manages employee lifecycle and HR operations.

Includes:
- Attendance
- Late marks
- Leaves
- Salary visibility
- Incentives
- Advances
- Role-based transparency
- Mobile-first experience

Employees can fully understand their employment status within the app.
All visibility rules are strict and hierarchical.

Refer SYSTEM_INTENT.md for enforcement logic.
