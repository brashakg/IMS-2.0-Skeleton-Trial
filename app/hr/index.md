# HR, Attendance & Payroll Screens Index

This file represents the root entry for all HR-related screens.

The HR module governs:

- Attendance tracking (geo-based and device-aware)
- Late marks and policy enforcement
- Paid and unpaid leave management
- Salary structure visibility
- Incentive calculation and breakdown
- Salary advance tracking
- Role-based transparency (employees see only their own data)
- Mobile-first access for all staff

HR data integrates with:
- Task and SOP enforcement
- Finance and payouts
- Performance dashboards

All HR rules, limits, and visibility constraints are defined in
`docs/SYSTEM_INTENT.md`.
