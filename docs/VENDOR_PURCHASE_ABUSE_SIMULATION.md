# VENDOR & PURCHASE ABUSE SIMULATION — IMS 2.0

## Purpose
Detect procurement inefficiencies and vendor misuse.

---

## PATTERN 1 — OVER-ORDERING LOOP

Pattern:
- Same vendor, same SKU repeatedly ordered

Detection:
- PO repetition clustering

Action:
- Inventory planning insight

---

## PATTERN 2 — GRN DISCREPANCY NORMALIZATION

Pattern:
- Small mismatches repeatedly ignored

Detection:
- GRN delta frequency

Action:
- Admin review

---

## PATTERN 3 — SINGLE-VENDOR DEPENDENCE

Pattern:
- Excessive dependency on one vendor

Detection:
- Vendor concentration ratio

Action:
- Procurement advisory

---

## GOVERNANCE
- ❌ No PO auto-block
- ✅ Negotiation & planning focus
