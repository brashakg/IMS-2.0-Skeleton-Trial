## Purpose
Make HR **transparent, predictable, and trusted** by staff.

Every employee must understand:
- Attendance
- Leave
- Salary
- Incentives
inside the system.

---

## ATTENDANCE RULES

- Geo-fenced login enforced
- Shift-based attendance
- Late marks auto-calculated

Manual attendance:
- Allowed only by Manager+
- Reason mandatory
- Logged permanently

---

## LEAVE GOVERNANCE

Leave types:
- Paid
- Unpaid
- Sick
- Emergency
- Special

Rules:
- Approval hierarchy enforced
- Backdated leave requires justification
- Leave balance auto-calculated

---

## PAYROLL PRINCIPLES

- Salary components visible to employee
- Deductions clearly itemized
- Advances auto-adjusted

Payroll once processed:
- Cannot be edited
- Correction via adjustment entry only

---

## INCENTIVES

- Rule-based
- Role-aware
- Period-locked

No manual incentive credit without approval.

---

## EMPLOYEE VISIBILITY

Employees can see:
- Own attendance
- Own leaves
- Own salary
- Own incentives
- Own advances

They cannot see others.

---

## AUDIT & FAIRNESS

- No silent edits
- No retroactive deletions
- Full history preserved
