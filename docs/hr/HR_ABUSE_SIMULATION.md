# HR ABUSE SIMULATION — IMS 2.0

## Purpose
Detect misuse without humiliating or accusing staff.

---

## PATTERN 1 — GHOST ATTENDANCE

Pattern:
- Perfect attendance but low activity

Detection:
- Attendance vs sales/tasks mismatch

Action:
- Flag to Manager
- No staff alert

---

## PATTERN 2 — SHIFT MISUSE

Pattern:
- Frequent shift changes to avoid penalties

Detection:
- Roster edit frequency

Action:
- Admin insight

---

## PATTERN 3 — INCENTIVE GAMING

Pattern:
- High-margin focus ignoring customer needs

Detection:
- Product mix analysis

Action:
- AI advisory (Superadmin only)

---

## PATTERN 4 — ADVANCE DEPENDENCY

Pattern:
- Frequent advances

Detection:
- Advance frequency & recovery delay

Action:
- Finance alert

---

## GOVERNANCE

- No auto-punishment
- No staff-facing AI
- Human decision final

---

END OF HR ABUSE SIMULATION
