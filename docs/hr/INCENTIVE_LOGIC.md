## Purpose
To remove confusion, disputes, and manipulation in incentives.

---

## 1. INCENTIVE STRUCTURE

Incentives can be based on:
- Product category
- Brand
- Margin
- Store performance
- Individual performance

Configured ONLY at HQ.

---

## 2. PRODUCT-BASED INCENTIVES

Each SKU can have:
- Fixed incentive
- Percentage incentive
- Slab-based incentive

Rules:
- Incentives defined at catalog level
- Cannot be changed at store

---

## 3. TARGET-BASED INCENTIVES

Targets can be:
- Monthly
- Quarterly

Shortfall handling:
- Partial incentive allowed (if enabled)
- Else zero

---

## 4. PENALTIES & BLOCKERS

Incentives blocked if:
- Attendance non-compliance
- Unresolved stock variance
- Policy breach

Block reason is visible to staff.

---

## 5. TRANSPARENCY RULE

System shows:
- How incentive was calculated
- What blocked it
- What can fix it

No silent deductions.
