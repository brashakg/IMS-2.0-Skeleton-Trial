# HR & ATTENDANCE RULES — IMS 2.0

## Purpose
This document defines how **people, time, money, and accountability** are handled.
The goal is transparency for staff and control for management.

No confusion. No hidden calculations. No manual side-books.

---

## 1. ATTENDANCE CAPTURE

Attendance can be captured via:
- Mobile phone (GPS + time)
- Tablet (store device)
- Desktop (store IP-restricted)

Rules:
- Login allowed only near assigned store (geo-fenced)
- Time is system-recorded (no manual override by staff)
- Offline entries allowed only with manager approval

---

## 2. SHIFT & ROSTER GOVERNANCE

Shifts are:
- Predefined by Superadmin / Admin
- Assigned store-wise
- Role-specific

Allowed actions:
- Store Manager can approve:
  - Shift swaps
  - Weekly offs
  - Emergency changes

System behavior:
- Once approved, roster auto-updates
- Attendance expectations change automatically

---

## 3. LATE MARKS & HALF DAY LOGIC

Rules:
- Late mark thresholds configurable in Superadmin
- Multiple late marks auto-convert to:
  - Half-day
  - Loss of incentive eligibility (configurable)

No silent deductions:
- Every late mark is visible to staff
- Reason can be added (optional)

---

## 4. LEAVE MANAGEMENT

Leave Types:
- Paid Leave
- Casual Leave
- Sick Leave
- Unpaid Leave
- Special Leave (HQ-defined)

Rules:
- Staff can only see their own balance
- Manager approves
- HR / Admin audits

System locks:
- Leave cannot be edited after payroll lock
- Retroactive leave requires Admin approval

---

## 5. SALARY VISIBILITY

Staff can see:
- Salary structure
- Monthly salary slip
- Attendance impact
- Deductions
- Incentives earned

Staff CANNOT:
- Edit salary
- See others’ salaries

---

## 6. ADVANCE & RECOVERY

Advance rules:
- Staff can request advances
- Approval required (Manager / Finance)
- Advance shown clearly as:
  - Amount
  - Recovery schedule
  - Remaining balance

Advance visibility:
- Staff (own only)
- Finance
- Admin / Superadmin

---

## 7. INCENTIVE ELIGIBILITY

Eligibility depends on:
- Attendance compliance
- No unresolved stock variance
- No disciplinary flags

Incentive calculation:
- Fully transparent
- Formula visible to staff
- No black-box numbers

---

## 8. EXIT & CLEARANCE RULES

Before exit:
- Attendance closure
- Stock responsibility clearance
- Advance recovery
- Task closure

System blocks exit completion until cleared.

---

## 9. AUDIT & LOGGING

Every HR action logs:
- Who
- When
- Why
- From where

No deletions.
Only append.

---

END OF HR & ATTENDANCE RULES
