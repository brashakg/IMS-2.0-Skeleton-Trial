# MARKETPLACE ABUSE & SLA SIMULATION — IMS 2.0

## Purpose
Detect operational, pricing, and fulfilment risks
WITHOUT blocking sales automatically.

---

## PATTERN 1 — PRICE LEAKAGE

Pattern:
- Marketplace selling below IMS-approved price

Detection:
- Real-time price comparison

Action:
- Sync paused
- Admin alerted
- Audit log created

---

## PATTERN 2 — INVENTORY DRAIN

Pattern:
- Marketplace orders draining stock disproportionately

Detection:
- Channel vs store allocation analysis

Action:
- Reduce marketplace allocation
- Suggest buffer increase

---

## PATTERN 3 — SLA BREACH

Pattern:
- Delayed dispatch or delivery

Detection:
- SLA clock tracking

Action:
- Task created
- Escalation ladder triggered

---

## PATTERN 4 — HIGH RETURNS

Pattern:
- Excessive returns from a channel

Detection:
- Return rate anomaly

Action:
- Admin insight
- Listing quality review suggested

---

## PATTERN 5 — MANUAL OVERRIDES

Pattern:
- Frequent manual sync overrides

Detection:
- Override frequency tracking

Action:
- Superadmin visibility
- Governance review suggested

---

## GOVERNANCE RULES

- ❌ No auto delisting
- ❌ No auto financial penalties
- ❌ No marketplace-facing alerts
- ✅ Read-only intelligence
- ✅ Human decision final
