# CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE FAILURE SIMULATION — IMS 2.0

## Purpose
Detect friction, neglect, or poor service BEFORE churn happens.

---

## PATTERN 1 — MULTIPLE VISITS, NO CONVERSION
Pattern:
- Customer visits repeatedly but does not purchase

Detection:
- Visit-to-sale ratio

Action:
- Manager insight
- Training suggestion

---

## PATTERN 2 — POST-SALE SILENCE
Pattern:
- No follow-up after payment

Detection:
- Payment vs communication gap

Action:
- Task auto-created

---

## PATTERN 3 — COMPLAINT LOOP
Pattern:
- Same customer raises multiple complaints

Detection:
- Complaint frequency

Action:
- Admin visibility

---

Governance:
- No staff alert
- No auto apology
