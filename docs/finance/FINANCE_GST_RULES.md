# FINANCE & GST RULES — IMS 2.0

## Purpose
To ensure statutory compliance, financial integrity, audit survivability, and zero ambiguity.

Finance data is **legally binding**.
Once locked, it cannot be casually changed.

---

## 1. FINANCIAL PERIOD GOVERNANCE

Financial periods:
- Monthly
- Quarterly
- Annual

Rules:
- Periods are locked by Accountant / Admin
- Once locked:
  - ❌ No edits
  - ❌ No back-dated entries
  - ❌ No deletions

Unlocking:
- Only Admin / Superadmin
- Mandatory reason
- Audit log created

---

## 2. GST ENTITY & REGISTRATION CONTROL

Rules:
- Each brand / company can have:
  - Multiple GST registrations
  - Across states

Invoices must:
- Use correct GSTIN based on store
- Apply correct CGST / SGST / IGST logic

GST rate source:
- Product category master
- Locked at catalog level

---

## 3. INVOICE FINALITY

Once invoice is generated:
- Prices cannot be edited
- GST values cannot be edited
- Line items cannot be changed

Corrections allowed only via:
- Credit Note
- Debit Note
- Return flows

---

## 4. PAYMENT MODES & RECONCILIATION

Supported modes:
- Cash
- UPI
- Card
- EMI
- Bank Transfer
- Gift Card
- Store Credit

Rules:
- Partial payments allowed
- Outstanding tracked automatically
- Mode-wise reconciliation mandatory

---

## 5. TILL CLOSING RULES

Daily till close:
- Mandatory
- Store-wise
- Cash variance must be explained

Rules:
- Till cannot be closed twice
- Till must be closed before next day sales
- Manager approval required for variance

---

## 6. EXPENSE BOOKING RULES

Expenses:
- Uploaded by staff
- Approved by hierarchy
- Visible to finance

Rules:
- Bill upload mandatory
- Category mandatory
- Date validation enforced
- Back-dated expenses require approval

---

## 7. ADVANCES & RECOVERY

Staff advances:
- Visible to staff (own only)
- Visible to Finance & Admin

Rules:
- Recovery schedule mandatory
- Salary auto-adjustment allowed
- Cannot close employment with pending advance

---

## 8. WRITE-OFFS & ADJUSTMENTS

Write-offs:
- Allowed only by Admin / Superadmin
- Reason mandatory
- Limit configurable

No silent adjustments allowed.

---

## 9. EXPORT & INTEGRATIONS

Exports:
- Tally
- GST returns

Rules:
- Export is logged
- Period must be locked
- No partial exports without trace

---

## 10. AUDIT & IMMUTABILITY

Finance logs are:
- Append-only
- Time-stamped
- Role-attributed

Nothing is deleted.
Only corrected through structured flows.

---

END OF FINANCE & GST RULES
