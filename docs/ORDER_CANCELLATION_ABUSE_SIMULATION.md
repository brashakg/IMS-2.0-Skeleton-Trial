# ORDER CANCELLATION ABUSE — IMS 2.0

## Purpose
Detect misuse of cancellations to manipulate stock or incentives.

---

## PATTERN 1 — POST-DISCOUNT CANCELLATION
Pattern:
- Orders cancelled after discount approval

Detection:
- Discount → cancel sequence

Action:
- Finance insight

---

## PATTERN 2 — STOCK HOLDING
Pattern:
- Orders created to reserve stock, then cancelled

Detection:
- Hold duration analysis

Action:
- Inventory review
