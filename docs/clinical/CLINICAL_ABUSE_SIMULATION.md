# CLINICAL ABUSE SIMULATION — IMS 2.0

## Purpose
Detect patterns that may indicate clinical shortcuts or misuse WITHOUT accusing professionals.

---

## PATTERN 1 — COPY-PASTE PRESCRIPTIONS

Pattern:
- Same values repeated across patients

Detection:
- Value similarity clustering

Action:
- Flag to Admin
- Review optometrist workload

---

## PATTERN 2 — UNUSUAL VALIDITY SETTINGS

Pattern:
- Always max validity used

Detection:
- Validity distribution analysis

Action:
- Admin insight

---

## PATTERN 3 — HIGH REDO RATE

Pattern:
- Frequent remakes linked to same optometrist

Detection:
- Redo ratio threshold

Action:
- Clinical review task

---

## PATTERN 4 — SALES-DRIVEN OVERRIDES

Pattern:
- Frequent prescription overrides near sales push

Detection:
- Override + time + sales correlation

Action:
- Superadmin visibility only

---

- ❌ No auto-blocking
- ❌ No optometrist alerts
- ✅ Read-only intelligence
